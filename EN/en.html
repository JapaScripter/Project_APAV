INSPIRED BY CHRISTIAN FAITH

The Project Association Reaching Lifes is dedicated to serving others, bringing God's love to the communities most in need.

In Heliópolis and other regions, we work tirelessly to improve the quality of life of families in vulnerable situations, offering food, maternal support and promoting social inclusion.

Our caravans bring hope to recovery houses, indigenous villages and needy communities in different countries. We believe that faith in action is capable of transforming lives and building a more promising future for everyone."

Mission

Promote the development and well-being of families in situations of social vulnerability, offering support, resources and training that enable them to build a dignified and self-sufficient life.
Our mission is to build a support network that strengthens community ties and contributes to reducing social inequalities. We seek to offer opportunities for growth and promote the autonomy of all the families we serve.

Vision

To be a national reference in assistance and support for families in vulnerable situations, contributing to the construction of a fairer, more inclusive and supportive society.
We aspire to a future where all families have access to rights and opportunities, overcoming social barriers and achieving a better quality of life.

Values

Empathy: We put ourselves in other people's shoes, seeking to understand and meet the specific needs of each family with respect and sensitivity.

Transparency: We act with clarity and responsibility in all actions, promoting trust and respect among families, partners and employees.

Social Commitment: We are dedicated to the cause of social transformation and work tirelessly to provide continuous improvement in the lives of the families we support.

Solidarity: We believe in the strength of the community and the power of unity to overcome challenges and build a more inclusive environment for everyone.

Resilience: We face obstacles with determination and encourage families to overcome their difficulties, offering continuous support adapted to each reality.
