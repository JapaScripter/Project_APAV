INSPIRED BY THE CHRISTIAN FAITH

The Projeto Alcançando Vidas Association is dedicated to serving others, bringing God's love to the neediest communities.

In Heliópolis and other regions, we work tirelessly to improve the quality of life of families in vulnerable situations, offering food, maternal support and promoting social inclusion.

Our caravans bring hope to recovery homes, indigenous villages and underprivileged communities in several countries. We believe that faith in action can transform lives and build a brighter future for everyone."

MISSION

Promote the development and well-being of families in situations of social vulnerability, offering support, resources and training that enable the construction of a dignified and self-sufficient life.

Our mission is to build a support network that strengthens community ties and contributes to reducing social inequalities. We seek to provide opportunities for growth and promote autonomy for all the families we serve.

VISION

To be a national reference in assistance and support for families in vulnerable situations, contributing to the construction of a more just, inclusive and supportive society.

We aspire to a future where all families have access to rights and opportunities, overcoming social barriers and achieving a better quality of life.

VALUES

Empathy: We put ourselves in the other person's shoes, seeking to understand and meet the specific needs of each family with respect and sensitivity.

Transparency: We act with clarity and responsibility in all actions, promoting trust and respect among families, partners and employees.

Solidarity: We believe in the strength of the community and the power of unity to overcome challenges and build a more inclusive environment for everyone.
